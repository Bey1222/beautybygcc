Refund Policy

This return and refund policy indicates the terms of eligibility for a return and/or a refund on your order. This policy covers our online sales. If you have any question that is not covered by this policy, you may contact our customer service and support team directly through Whatsapp.

How many days do I have to return my item?

We understand that you might change your mind about your purchase. As such, you can return any item ordered free of charge, within 2 days of delivery. The 2-day window starts on the day you receive the item at your delivery location. Notify us of your intent to return, please contact our customer support team through email or whatsapp. Once we are informed of your intent to return your order, you will have 2 days to send us back the item from the date of your return notification to us. You do not have to provide any reason for the return. However, we would love to get more feedback on the reason for your return so we may contact you to learn a bit more about your experience. This will help us improve our product and the experience for our customers.

Do I need to cover the return shipping cost?

For all orders you will have to cover the price of the return shipping.

What type of refund can I expect?

If you return your order within the return window mentioned above (2 days after you receive the item), we would allow you pick out another product with a discount, and if you do want a refund you must wait until product is re-sold.

When will I receive my refund?

Typically, you can expect to receive your refund within 2 days of when we receive your refund request. Your wait time may be longer if we haven’t received the item back from you.

How much will the refund be?
